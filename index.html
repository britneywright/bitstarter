FAQ

What happens if I support a business that doesn't win?
If the business you support doesn't win your credit card will not be charged.
How will I receieve my reward?
When the campaign ends rewards from the winning business will be sent to you via email. You can print the rewards or store them on your phone or tablet for use at the pop up event.
Can I give rewards as gifts.
Yes. You will be prompted to share your rewards during checkout if you'd like yto.
I can't make it to the event. Can I give my reward to someone else?
Yes. You can forward your reward to another person if you cannnot attend the event.
